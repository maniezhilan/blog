and clicking on my Sound blaster X-Fi and selecting the profile as Analog Surround 5.1 output , also go to the output and select your device.

1.Enabled sound as mentioned above.(People with NVidia, do not upgrade ubuntu through synaptic ,it breaks without warning ).

2.Installed Gnome-do through synaptic (can't work without gnome-do for linux and quicksilver for mac).

3.Enabled Micro-blogging plugin from the gnome-do preferences (make sure you select all plugins) for twitter.

4.Installed screen sudo apt-get install screen , ubuntu byobu is too good for people who want to multitask with command lines.

5.Installed chrome browser aka crossover chromium.
