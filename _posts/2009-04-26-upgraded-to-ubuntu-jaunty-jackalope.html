The reason being i have used ubuntu from Gutsy, hardy and interpid. All the above release had some glitches with my crazy architecture (AMD64 running on VMWare Server on vista home basic , partition on my external hard disk plugged through an USB port).

The upgrade was easy , but this time it was slow may be too many users downloading it.

I always had trouble with flash player on AMD64, but in jaunty it works like an charm. Oh hoo ..youtube for my wife on Ubuntu.

Visual Effects never used to work for my Nvidia , but with jaunty i get custom visual effects.

The booting is lightning fast.

Lot of themes to choose from , i chose the Dust, its awesome.
